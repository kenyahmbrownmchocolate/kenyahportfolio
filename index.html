Our Story

House of Kenyariri began with a passion for transforming spaces into meaningful environments that tell a story. Founded on principles of purpose-driven design, we create interiors that reflect both functionality and personality.

Our approach combines timeless aesthetics with thoughtful details, ensuring each space we touch becomes a true reflection of those who inhabit it. Through careful curation and intentional design, we help our clients discover beauty in the everyday.
